Gardening Tips

💧

Water Smartly

Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation and help roots absorb better.

🌿

Use Compost

Enrich your soil with organic compost to promote healthy plant growth.

☀️

Sunlight is Key

Place your plants in locations where they get 4-6 hours of sunlight daily.

✂️

Prune Regularly

Trimming dead leaves and branches encourages fresh and bushier growth.

🗣️

Talk to Plants

Interacting with your plants creates a calm environment and supports growth!
